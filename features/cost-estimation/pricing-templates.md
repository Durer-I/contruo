# Pricing Templates

> **Category:** Cost & Estimation
> **Priority:** P1 - Post-Launch
> **Status:** Brainstorming

## Overview

Create and manage reusable pricing templates for common assemblies and trades. Templates bundle multiple cost items together so that when a takeoff condition is measured, all associated costs are automatically calculated. Reduces repetitive data entry across projects.

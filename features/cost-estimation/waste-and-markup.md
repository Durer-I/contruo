> **Category:** Cost & Estimation

## Overview

Apply waste percentages, overhead costs, profit margins, and contingency factors to estimates. Supports per-item, per-trade, and project-wide factor application. Allows estimators to accurately account for real-world material waste and business costs in their pricing.

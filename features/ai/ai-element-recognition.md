# AI Element Recognition

> **Category:** AI Features
> **Priority:** P1 - Post-Launch
> **Status:** Brainstorming

## Overview

Identify and classify construction elements from plan sheets including doors, windows, walls, fixtures, MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) components, and structural elements. Uses trained models to recognize standard construction symbols and annotations, providing a foundation for automated takeoff and quantity generation.

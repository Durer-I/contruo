> **Category:** AI Features
> **Priority:** P2 - Future

## Overview

Suggest quantities and assemblies based on AI analysis of plan content. After elements are recognized, the AI recommends appropriate conditions, assemblies, and material quantities based on the plan context, project type, and historical data from similar projects. Reduces the estimator's workload from manual configuration.

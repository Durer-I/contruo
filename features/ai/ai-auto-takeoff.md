# AI Auto-Takeoff

> **Category:** AI Features
> **Priority:** P1 - Post-Launch
> **Status:** Brainstorming

## Overview

Automatically detect and measure elements from construction plans using computer vision and AI. Analyzes uploaded plan sheets to identify measurable elements (walls, rooms, pipes, etc.) and generates takeoff measurements without manual tracing. Estimators review and refine the AI-generated results rather than starting from scratch.

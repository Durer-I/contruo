# AI Cost Estimation

> **Category:** AI Features
> **Priority:** P2 - Future
> **Status:** Brainstorming

## Overview

Auto-suggest pricing based on historical project data, market rates, and regional cost indices. Analyzes past estimates and industry pricing databases to recommend unit costs for materials, labor rates, and equipment costs. Helps estimators price items faster while staying competitive and accurate.
